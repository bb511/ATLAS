## Final Value
Finally, we quote the final value for the cross section obtained in the $Z\rightarrow\mu\mu$ process to be
\begin{equation}
\sigma_{Z\mu\mu} = 1.20\pm 0.01 \mathrm{(stat)}\pm 0.12 \mathrm{(syst)}\pm 0.02 \mathrm{(lumi)}
\end{equation}
