## Final Value
Finally, we quote the final value for the cross section obtained in the $W\rightarrow\mu\nu$ process to be
\begin{equation}
\sigma_{W\mu\nu} = 12.35\pm 0.03 \mathrm{(stat)}\pm 0.47 \mathrm{(syst)}\pm 0.02 \mathrm{(lumi)}
\end{equation}
